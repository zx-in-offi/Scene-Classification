ResNet50

Parameters: ~25.6M Size: ~90 MB

A classic Convolutional Neural Network architecture that uses residual connections to allow training of deep networks without suffering from the vanishing gradient problem. It provides a very strong baseline and high accuracy.

EfficientNet-B0

Parameters: ~5.3M Size: ~15 MB

A highly optimized architecture that uniformly scales network width, depth, and resolution. Despite being significantly smaller and faster than ResNet50, it achieves comparable or even better accuracy, making it perfect for efficient deployment.

Vision Transformer (ViT)

Parameters: ~86M

An architecture that applies the Transformer mechanism (originally designed for NLP) to image classification. It splits images into patches and processes them using self-attention. While powerful, it is much larger and wasn't selected for this live deployment.
